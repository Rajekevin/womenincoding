Women in Coding

Providing affordable coding classes for women in the community

Women in Coding was started in early 2016 by Zoe Koulouris and Kseniya Lifanova. Our mission is to provide resources and opportunities for those interested in coding and design in the local community.

Although this group is geared towards women, anyone is welcome to attend our classes.

We host affordable monthly classes as well as casual events for people to network and connect. Please see below for our next class.
